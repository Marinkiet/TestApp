# 📋 Complete Feature List

## 🎯 Core Financial Features

### Income Management
- ✅ **Salary Selector**: Choose between best case (R29,500), worst case (R28,300), or custom amount
- ✅ **Additional Income Streams**: Add unlimited income sources with dates
- ✅ **Real-time Totals**: Automatic calculation of total monthly income
- ✅ **Income History**: View all income sources with dates

### Expense Management
- ✅ **Add Expenses**: Create new expenses with name, amount, category, and notes
- ✅ **Edit Expenses**: Modify any expense details
- ✅ **Delete Expenses**: Remove expenses you no longer have
- ✅ **Move Expenses**: Transfer between fixed and variable categories
- ✅ **Category System**: Organize by Housing, Transportation, Utilities, Food, etc.
- ✅ **Expense Notes**: Add context to variable expenses
- ✅ **Automatic Totals**: Real-time calculation of fixed and variable totals

### Unexpected Transactions
- ✅ **Log Income**: Track bonuses, gifts, refunds, side gigs
- ✅ **Log Expenses**: Track repairs, medical bills, emergencies
- ✅ **Date Tracking**: Record when transactions occurred
- ✅ **Impact Analysis**: See how unexpected items affect your balance
- ✅ **Separate Tracking**: Keep unexpected items separate from regular budget

### Summary Dashboard
- ✅ **Total Income Card**: Shows all income sources combined
- ✅ **Total Expenses Card**: Shows all spending combined
- ✅ **Net Balance Card**: Surplus or deficit with color coding
- ✅ **Debt Card**: Current credit card balance
- ✅ **Real-time Updates**: All cards update instantly with changes
