## 🚀 How to Use

### Opening the Menu
1. Tap the **burger icon** (☰) in top left
2. Menu slides in smoothly
3. See all 9 sections

### Navigating
1. Tap any section in menu
2. Smooth scroll to that section
3. Menu closes automatically
4. Active section highlighted

### Closing the Menu
- Tap **X** button in menu
- Tap **outside** the menu
- Tap **any menu item**
- Natural and intuitive
