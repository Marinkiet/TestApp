# 🚀 Quick Start Guide

## Installation & Setup

### 1. Install Dependencies
```bash
npm install
```

### 2. Start the App
```bash
npm run dev
```

The app will open at `http://localhost:3000`

## First Time Setup

### Step 1: Set Your Salary
1. Go to "Income Management" section
2. Select your salary scenario:
   - Best Case: R 29,500
   - Worst Case: R 28,300
   - Custom: Enter your own amount

### Step 2: Review Your Expenses
Your expenses are pre-loaded with your data:
- **Fixed Expenses**: Rent, car, utilities, etc.
- **Variable Expenses**: Payflex, tablet payment

**You can:**
- ✏️ Edit any expense amount
- ➕ Add new expenses
- 🗑️ Delete expenses
- ↔️ Move between fixed/variable

### Step 3: Track Unexpected Transactions
Click "+ Add Transaction" to log:
- Unexpected income (bonuses, gifts, refunds)
- Unexpected expenses (car repairs, medical bills)

### Step 4: Start Tracking Income Ideas
1. Choose an income idea (e.g., Freelance Development)
2. Click "Start Tracking"
3. Log your progress:
   - Time spent (hours)
   - Money invested (R)
   - Money generated (R)
   - Notes about what you did

### Step 5: Set Your Goals
**Credit Card Debt:**
- Current: R 30,000
- Enter payment amounts as you pay
- Track progress toward debt-free

**Emergency Fund:**
- Goal: R 30,000
- Add money as you save
- Build your safety net

### Step 6: Track Personal Development
**Gym Tracker:**
- Log workout sessions
- Set monthly goal (default: 12 sessions)
- Track progress

**Language Learning:**
- Log study sessions
- Set monthly goal (default: 30 minutes)
- Track multiple languages

### Step 7: Generate Monthly Reports
At month-end:
1. Go to "Monthly Report Generator"
2. Click "Generate PDF Report"
3. Review your progress and recommendations

## Common Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to any server.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: Yes! The dashboard is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers.

**Q: What if I clear my browser data?**
A: Your financial data will be lost. Consider exporting reports regularly as backup.

**Q: Can I track multiple income ideas?**
A: Yes! Track as many as you want. Each idea has its own progress tracking.

**Q: How do I move an expense from fixed to variable?**
A: Click the "Move to Variable" button on any fixed expense (or vice versa).

**Q: Can I change my monthly goals?**
A: Yes! Click on the goal number in Gym Tracker or Language Tracker to edit.
