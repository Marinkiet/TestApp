## 🎯 How It Works

### Earning Points
Every positive action earns points:
- Make a debt payment → Earn points
- Save money → Earn points
- Generate income → Earn points
- Reduce expenses → Earn points
- Log workouts → Earn points
- Study languages → Earn points
- Track consistently → Earn points
- Generate reports → Earn points

### Unlocking Achievements
Achievements unlock automatically when you hit milestones:
- Pay off R1,000 in debt → **Debt Crusher** unlocked!
- Save R5,000 → **Financial Cushion** unlocked!
- Generate R1,000 income → **Money Maker** unlocked!
- 10 workouts → **Week Warrior** unlocked!

### Leveling Up
Accumulate points to level up:
- Reach 500 points → Level 2: Apprentice
- Reach 1,000 points → Level 3: Practitioner
- Reach 10,000 points → Level 8: Titan
